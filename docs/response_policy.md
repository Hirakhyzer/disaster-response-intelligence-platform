# Human-control and response policy

## Core principle

This platform produces analysis and planning evidence only. It does not issue official alerts, dispatch resources, close roads, order evacuations, or instruct the public.

## Allowed use in this repository

- Generate synthetic hazard and exposure scenarios.
- Rank fictional priority zones.
- Estimate fictional resource demand.
- Produce local maps and planning tables.
- Create a briefing for research demonstration.
- Explore reproducibility, auditability, and methodology.

## Not allowed as a software action

- Public warning or evacuation order.
- Real dispatch of personnel or vehicles.
- Official shelter-opening decisions.
- Road-closure or route-navigation instruction.
- Claims of live flood, wildfire, or weather conditions.
- Claims of official government, NGO, or emergency-service authority.

## Official review requirement

Any real-world deployment would require validated data feeds, official operating procedures, responsible agencies, legal authority, accessibility review, privacy review, and human incident-command approval.
